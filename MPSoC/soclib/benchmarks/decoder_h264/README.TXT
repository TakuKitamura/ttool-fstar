----------------------------
- Structure of directories -
----------------------------

- common
  Contains sources common to both the caba and tlmdt platforms.

- caba_platform
  Platform at the caba level. This platform can support up to 6 processors.

- tlmdt_platform
  On the same model as the caba platform but with tlmdt components.

- simple_tlmdt_platform
  Platform to be used by soclib beginner. This platlform is less configurable.
